About The Event

AgriMind 2026 is a two-day innovation summit focused on smart irrigation, AI-powered farming, climate-resilient agriculture, and sustainable water management. The event brings together students, developers, researchers, and innovators to discuss how technology can solve modern agricultural challenges.

Smart Irrigation

Explore how AI and IoT help reduce water wastage in agriculture.

Future Farming

Discover modern farming techniques using automation and analytics.

Sustainability

Learn about eco-friendly innovations shaping the future of food production.
